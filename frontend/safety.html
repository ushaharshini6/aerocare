Flight Safety Tips & Encouragement

Flying can be stressful, but staying calm is your best tool. Listen carefully to the crew and follow their instructions.

When you board, find the nearest emergency exits and count the rows to them. Knowing your way out helps you stay prepared.

In an emergency, don’t worry about your belongings. Leave everything behind and move quickly but steadily toward the exits.

If there’s smoke, stay low to breathe cleaner air, cover your nose and mouth with a cloth, and follow the signs and crew directions.
